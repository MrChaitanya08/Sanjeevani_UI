About Sanjeevani Hospital
Care beyond Cure

Established in 1999 with the sole aim of providing excellent medical treatment at affordable price Sanjeevani surgical and General Hospital is the brain child of its Founder and Medical Director Dr. Sunil Agrawal. The name "SANJEEVANI" is inspired from the name of Dr. Sunil Agrawal’s mother & also the life saving herb in Ayurveda.

Sanjeevani hospital aspires to be a highly effective professional organization devoted to ensuring and striving to improve the health status of our community.

Cancer Care

Cancer is treated through Chemotherapy, Radiation therapy, or Surgical Oncology. We focus on Comprehensive Cancer care in all sub-specialities and are supervised by Eminent Panel of Experts.

Cardiac Care

At Sanjeevani, we will treat your heart problems while staying aware of other conditions, including high blood pressure, high Cholesterol, diabetes and other factors that risk damaging the heart.

Covid Treatment

With around 100 beds and 18 ICU facilities, we provide excellent all-round care for all Covid patients and ensure that all protocols are deligently followed for faster relief.

Maternity Home

If you are planning for a baby, you will want to have the best of everything and it starts with your choice of doctors and hospitals. This is why many couples choose to have their baby at "Sanjeevani hospital."
